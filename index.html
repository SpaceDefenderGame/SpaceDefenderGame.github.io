Features

Absolutely responsive

You can play this game on any device with screen resolution from 320*568 pixels

Simple control

You can use up, down, left and right buttons on your keybord, mouse, touchpad or control the spaceship with the touch of a finger on a touch screen

Shop into the game

You can earn bitcoins and buy more health or improve your weapons

Smart opponents

They are really very smart! They run away when you shoot them and will not let you get too close to them

10 levels

Each level has its own graphic and music background. And it's harder and harder to kill enemies.

Phaser.js

A really fantastic game framework which helped make this game
